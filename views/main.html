About the problem

Our problem is set in the planet of Lengaburu...in the distant galaxy of Tara B. After the recent war with neighbouring planet Falicornia, King Shan has exiled the Queen of Falicornia for 15 years.

Queen Al Falcone is now in hiding. But if King Shan can find her before the years are up, she will be exiled for another 15 years....

How do we help King Shan ??

King Shan has received intelligence that Al Falcone is in hiding in one of these 6 planets - DonLon, Enchai, Jebing, Sapir, Lerbin & Pingasor. However he has limited resources at his disposal & can send his army to only 4 of these planets.

Now let us help King Shan in deploying his army to find queen AI Falcone.

How UI works

Out of 6 planets, you need to select any 4 planets.

Select which vehicle to be sent to selected planet.

Every vehicle has its speed and distance range. At each vehicle selection time will be calculated and disaplyed.

You can change planet or vehicle n times before submit.

Once all the 4 planets and vehicles are selected, submit button will be enabled. Once you click submit, the result will be displayed in next view.
